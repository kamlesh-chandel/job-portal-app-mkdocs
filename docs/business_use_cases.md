| # | Business Use Case               | Description                                                                   | Stakeholders         |
| - | ------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------------------- |
| 1 | Recruiters manage job postings  | Recruiters can create, edit, and delete job listings.                         | Recruiters           |
| 2 | Students apply for jobs         | Students browse job listings and apply with a resume and cover letter.        | Students             |
| 3 | Application review and decision | Recruiters review applications and update status (accept/reject).             | Recruiters, Students |
| 4 | Profile management              | Students maintain updated resumes, skills, and contact info.                  | Students             |
| 5 | Job discovery                   | Students can search and filter jobs by title, company, or location.           | Students             |
| 6 | Secure role-based system        | Authentication and authorization to ensure correct access control.            | All users            |
| 7 | Transparency in hiring          | Students can see job status updates ("Pending", “Under Review”, "Interview Scheduled", "Hired", “Rejected”). | Students             |
