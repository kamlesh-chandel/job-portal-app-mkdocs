# User Roles
In this application, there are two main roles: **Student** and **Recruiter**.  
Each role has specific features and permissions that define what they can do inside the system.

---

## 1. Student Role

### Description:
Students are job seekers who use this platform to explore and apply for job opportunities.

### Main Functionalities:
- **Account Access:** Create an account or log in securely.
- **View Jobs:** Access all active job listings posted by recruiters.
- **Search Jobs:** Search for jobs by title, company, or location.
- **View Job Details:** See full job information before applying.
- **Apply for Jobs:** Submit an application and upload a resume.
- **Profile Management:** Update resume, skills, and personal information.
- **Track Applications:** View all applied jobs with status updates like *Pending*, *Under Review*, *Interview Scheduled*, *Hired* or *Rejected*.

---

## 2. Recruiter Role

### Description:
Recruiters are users responsible for posting jobs and managing student applications.

### Main Functionalities:
- **Account Access:** Login or register securely as a recruiter.
- **Post Jobs:** Create new job listings with required details.
- **View Applicants:** See a list of all students who applied to their jobs.
- **Resume Review:** Access student resumes directly through the dashboard.
- **Updating Application Status:** Update the application status for each applicant.
- **Manage Posted Jobs:** View all their active job posts in one dashboard.

---

This clear role separation ensures data privacy, secure access, and a smooth experience for both types of users.
