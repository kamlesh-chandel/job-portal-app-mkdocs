# Workflow
This section explains how the system works for both **Students** and **Recruiters**, showing the main flow of actions step-by-step.

---

## Student Workflow

1. **Registration / Login:**  
   The student creates an account or logs in securely.

2. **Browse Jobs:**  
   Once logged in, they can view all available job posts listed by recruiters.

3. **Search Job:**  
   Students can search for specific jobs based on keywords.

4. **Job Detail Page:**
   Students can visit job detail page for detail information of particular job.

4. **Apply for a Job:**  
   After visiting job detail page, students can apply for the job.

5. **Profile Management:**  
   Students can update their skills and resume anytime through their profile section.

6. **Track Application Status:**  
   After applying, the student can view the job application status as *Pending*, *Under Review*, *Interview Scheduled*, *Hired* or *Rejected*.

---

## Recruiter Workflow

1. **Registration / Login:**  
   Recruiter signs up or logs in securely.

2. **Post Job:**  
   Recruiter creates a new job post with details like title, description, experience, and location.

3. **Manage Jobs:**  
   Recruiter can see their posted jobs at any time.

4. **View Applicants:**  
   The system lists all students who have applied for each job post.

5. **Review Applications:**  
   Recruiter checks resumes and decides the states of the application.

6. **Status Update:**  
   Recruiter updates the application status, and the student can view it in their dashboard.

---

This workflow ensures both users interact smoothly and have clear visibility of all actions taken throughout the recruitment process.
